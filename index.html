Our Story

A Legacy of Elegance in Deoghar

Situated at the heart of Azad Chowk, Deoghar — the sacred city of Baba Baidyanath — Nayasha (4N Mall) is Jharkhand's most beloved destination for premium ethnic and readymade fashion. For years, we have dressed the families of Deoghar for their most special moments: weddings, festivals, pujas, and milestones. Our commitment to quality, variety, and affordability has made us the first name in fashion for thousands of customers across the Santhal Parganas.
