# Nayasha - Luxury Ethnic Wear

Welcome to Nayasha, India's finest boutique for premium ethnic fashion. 

## Our Selection
We specialize in providing high-quality, handcrafted garments including:
- **Sarees**: Authentic Banarasi and ready-to-wear sarees.
- **Lehengas**: Exclusive Bridal wear and Party Lehengas.
- **Menswear**: Tailored Sherwanis and Coat-Pants.
- **Modern**: Indo-Western outfits and Crop tops.

## Shopping Experience
- **100% Authentic**: Every piece is carefully verified.
- **Shipping**: Free delivery on orders above ₹2999 across India.
- **Returns**: Minimal friction with a 7-day hassle-free return policy.
- **Payments**: Secure processing with support for UPI, Cards, Net Banking, and COD.

## Storefront Architecture
The Nayasha storefront is a highly optimized, state-of-the-art Single Page Application (SPA) driven by HTML, responsive CSS, and native JavaScript. It embeds a bespoke interconnected cart, wishlisting, dynamic live filtering mechanics, and introduces an immersive 3D WebGL viewing dimension for exploring fashion pieces.
